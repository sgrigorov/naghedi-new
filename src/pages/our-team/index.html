Welcome to NAGHEDI Immigration Inc, our world of immigration, refugee, and citizenship law practice and advocacy!

Sara Naghedi-Ahmadi, B.A., G.DipICL

  • Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant and a licensed counsel for Immigration and Refugee Board of Canada, RCIC-IRB.

  • Member in good standing of the College of Citizenship and Immigration Consultants, CICC - formerly known as ICCRC.

  • Member in good standing of the Canadian Association of Professional Immigration Consultants, CAPIC.

  • Associate member of the Association of Translators and Interpreters of Alberta (ATIA).

I am a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant. I graduated from the Faculty of Law at Queen’s University in Canada with a specialization in Immigration and Citizenship Law. As a licensed counsel for the Immigration and Refugee Board of Canada (IRB), I am also authorized and honored to represent our clients in Appeals, Admissibility Hearings, Detention Reviews, and Removal Order Reviews before all four divisions of the Immigration and Refugee Board of Canada, when necessary. I've lived in Canada for nearly two decades as an immigrant myself, holding a bachelor’s degree in music, as well!

My journey into immigration law began in 2013, fueled by my passion to harmonize diverse voices of the indigenous communities and Canada's earlier settlers with recent arrivals and those aspiring to call Canada home.

In my varied roles as an Immigration Consultant (RCIC - IRB), Settlement Counselor, and Community and Court Interpreter in Canada, I've had the opportunity to assist diverse asylum seekers and immigrants with the legal processes of their immigration, refugee claims, settlement, and integration. These experiences have reinforced my belief in the transformative power of immigration and the importance of creating pathways to success for persevering individuals and families.

In founding NAGHEDI Immigration Inc., I aim to continue my mission of providing professional, personalized, and compassionate assistance to those navigating the complexities of immigration and refugee journeys. I believe that together, we can build a brighter, more inclusive future for everyone who strives to make the world a better place to live for themselves and others.

Farinaz Razi, M.A., LL.B.

  • Canadian Lawyer

  • Member of the Law Society of Alberta

I am a Canadian lawyer who earned my bachelor’s degree in Law in 2012 from the University of Calgary in Canada. Following my graduation, I contributed to Indigenous Residential School claims at Kathleen Mahoney Law, which remains one of the significant legal litigations in Canada to date.

I was called to the Alberta Bar in 2014 and showcased my expertise in both the Alberta Court of Justice and the Court of King’s Bench, specializing in Judicial Reviews and Appeals. Between 2018 and 2021, I operated Razi Law, my sole proprietorship practice, in Calgary, Alberta.

Inspired by my contributions and diverse legal experience during my articling at Calgary Legal Guidance, Calgary Women’s Association, and the United Nations Calgary Chapter, I always emphasized client education, efficiency, and affordability during my practice.

My legal expertise spans areas such as Family Law, Child Protection, Domestic Violence, Criminal Law, and Residential Tenancy Law.

Farinaz is currently living abroad. Nonetheless, her position as a member of the Law Society of Alberta, coupled with her deep and extensive knowledge of the Canadian legal system, multifaceted experience, and unwavering dedication reflect her commitment to legal excellence and motivated her to join our team at NAGHEDI.

We are committed to recruit our knowledge and expertise to empower your journey!
